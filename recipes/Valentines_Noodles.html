Valentine's Spicy Peanut Noodles

A mouthwatering combination of spicy, sweet, salty, and nutty, this dish is best prepared on a cold night with friends. Serves 4-6 people.

Ingredients

    Sauce Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter. Chunky or creamy doesn't matter, choose whichever you prefer.
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce.
  • 14 oz. coconut milk. Just go for the canned stuff.
  • 1/2 cup agave nectar.
  • 1/4 cup sirracha sauce.
  • 1/4 cup lime juice.
  • Garlic Powder. I use garlic powder instead of raw garlic mainly so it integrates better into the sauce.
  • Red Chili Flakes.
  • Crushed Pepper.
    Dry Ingredients
  • A 16 oz package of rice noodles.
  • A few stalks of Celery. Slice these pretty thin.
  • 5-6 carrots, sliced at an angle around 1/8 of an inch thin.
  • 1 Block of Extra firm Tofu, diced into one inch cubes.
  • A whole Onion, diced.
  • A head of garlic, diced finely. I usually go with two or three because I'm a freak.
  • One red and one green bell pepper, diced.
  • Some broccoli, if you want.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
