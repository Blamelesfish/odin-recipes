Wholesome Shepherd's Pie

This wholesome, rich shepherd's pie is a fantastic meal for a cold night in fall, or after a long day's work. Sit back with a nice dark beer and a bowl full of this steaming hot pie.

Ingredients

  • One and a half to two pounds of potatoes, peeled and quartered.
  • Eight tablespoons of butter.
  • One medium onion, chopped. (About half a cup.)
  • 1-2 cups of mixed diced peas, carrots and corn.
  • One and a half pounds of ground beef.
  • Half a cup of beef broth.
  • One teaspoon of worcestershire sauce.
  • Salt, pepper, and other seasonings of choice.
